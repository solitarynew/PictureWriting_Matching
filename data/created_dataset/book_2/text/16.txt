Percy was a lieutenant in the army. He wore a dark green uniform and a matching beret. Percy had fought in a war before. He was well respected for his bravery.
Percy trained hard every day.  He took part in numerous field exercises.  He wore a helmet and he carried a haversack that was packed with army equipment. He also carried a rifle. The other soldiers often turned to Percy for advice. They also liked him to tell stories about the time he fought in the war.
Percy liked his job very much but he was also looking forward to his retirement. He would help out at a home for the elderly then.
