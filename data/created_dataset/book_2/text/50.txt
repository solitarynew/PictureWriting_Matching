Ah Meng is a monkey. It has a brown and hairy body and a long tail. It lives in the zoo. There are big trees with many branches for it to swing from in its enclosure. Its long tail is useful for swinging from tree to tree.
Ah Meng likes to eat bananas and nuts. It can swing from its tail while eating a banana. It will throw the banana skin on the ground after eating. Sometimes, Ah Meng performs tricks when many visitors come to its enclosure. It will swing quickly from one tree to another. It is certainly agile.
Ah Meng often chatters noisily to the other monkeys in the enclosure. The chattering becomes especially loud when they are excited. They sound like they are talking to one another. Many people like to watch Ah Meng and the other monkeys. The enclosure is always surrounded by visitors.
