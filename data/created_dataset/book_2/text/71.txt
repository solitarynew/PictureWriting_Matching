Computers come in many shapes and sizes.The most commonly used ones are personal computers. They are used at home and in the office. There are also small portable computers known as laptops. The even smaller ones are called palmtops.
Personal computers are big and bulky but they are also the easiest to use. Laptops are smaller and more stylish.  Many people who travel have one. It is convenient because they can use it wherever they go. Palmtops cannot store as much information as personal computers or laptops. However, they are handy and can fit into pockets.
Computers are useful in the world today. Everyone, from pupils to adults, rely on the computer daily. Our lives would be very different without these computers.
