Jeff Bricks is a well-known magician. Both the young and old have heard of him. They enjoy watching him perform. He has a new trick at every performance.
Jeff does not limit his performances to indoor tricks. He performs many tricks outdoors too. He can make an entire building disappear.  Once, he even walked through the Great Wall of China. That performance was broadcast in many countries all over the world. Jeff Bricks stunned everyone. He had done something that was impossible.
No other magician is as good as Jeff Bricks.  He can do what everyone would consider impossible. Everyone cannot wait for his next performance. He will make an aeroplane disappear.
