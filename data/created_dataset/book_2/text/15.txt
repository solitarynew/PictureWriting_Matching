Jack is an extremely busy boy. He occupies himself with many activities every day. He has many interests and is always willing to try new things.
Jack enjoys painting. He attends classes at the nearby community centre every Saturday.  His paintings are always bright and colourful. Jack also plays the piano. He has lessons on Saturdays but he practises every day. He is taking part in a competition next year. Jack also likes playing computer games. He is good at them but he makes sure that he does not spend too much time playing games. When he has the time, he likes to sing. He has a melodious voice.
Before Jack goes to bed, he reads a storybook. He finishes a book every night. He hopes to write his own story one day. As an author, he will be able to think of interesting characters that live in fascinating worlds.
