Isabella is a petite girl. She has a sweet smile and two dimples on her cheek. However, do not judge a book by its cover.
Isabella is known for her mischief. She likes to scream and shout in school. She also likes to play tricks on her classmates. Isabella often gets scolded by her teachers. They also complain to her parents. However, no matter how harsh they are, she still continues to behave badly. She is not sorry for what she has done.
At home, Isabella always annoys her younger brother and makes him cry. Everybody hopes that she will turn over a new leaf one day.
