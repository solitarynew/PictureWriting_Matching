The telephone is one of the greatest inventions in the world. It allows us to contact one another easily.
The telephone helps to transfer information from one person to another quickly. What we say is instantly heard by the person at the other end.
Now, we can even talk as we move around. These telephones are called mobile phones.  They are handy and lightweight. There are many different designs. Some are quite cheap. Others cost a few thousand dollars.  With improvements in technology, mobile phones can do more than just allow us to speak to each other. They can also allow us to surf the Internet and play games.
Today, many of us cannot do without a telephone. It helps us to stay in touch with people at all times no matter where we are.
